The following versions of MDS are available:

MDS in GT4 | MDS in GT3 | MDS in GT2

MDS4 is a WSRF implementation of information services released with Globus Toolkit 4.0. This version of MDS includes WSRF implementations of the Index Service, a Trigger Service, WebMDS (formerly known as the Web Service Data Browser) and the underyling framework, the Aggregator Framework.

To support existing deployments, the GT 4.0 distribution also includes MDS2 (also referred to as Pre-Web Services MDS).
