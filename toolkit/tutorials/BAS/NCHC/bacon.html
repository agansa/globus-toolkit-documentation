Charles Bacon has delivered numerous tutorials worldwide on the Globus Toolkit, including IEEE's SuperComputing conferences, the Global Grid Forum, and Globusworld conferences. He is a co-author of several toolkit guides and tutorials, and is an active participant in Globus community forums. Bacon serves as the Technology Coordinator for Packaging for the Globus Toolkit, and is the lead developer for matters relating to GT Administration. He is a staff member of the University of Chicago and a member of the Distributed Systems Laboratory in the Mathematics and Computer Science Division of Argonne National Lab.
