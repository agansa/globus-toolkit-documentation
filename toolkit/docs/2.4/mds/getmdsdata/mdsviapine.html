Many e-mail clients provide a means to access an LDAP directory. Since MDS uses OpenLDAP to contain Grid resource information, you can access MDS data from within these mail clients.

The practical application of doing this may be suspect, but the purpose here is to demonstrate the broad range of options available for consuming and making use of the Grid resource information.
