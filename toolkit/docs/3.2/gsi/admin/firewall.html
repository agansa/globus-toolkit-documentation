Firewall Requirements for the Globus Toolkit and GSI

The following pdf document provides requirements and guidance to firewall administrators at sites that are deploying the Globus Toolkit (version 2 or 3) clients and/or services. It describes the network traffic generated by using the Globus Toolkit, both in terms of what ports are used by what clients and services and the nature of the traffic in terms of authentication of connections and message protection of the data.
