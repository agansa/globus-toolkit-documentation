We're happy to announce the alpha version of the Globus Documentation Project (GDP), a community website that will serve to complement the official Globus documentation. Its purpose is to act as an open space where members of the Globus community can share their common knowledge on the Globus Toolkit, and to exchange all sorts of documentation resources. Thanks go to Borja Sotomayor for making this happen.
