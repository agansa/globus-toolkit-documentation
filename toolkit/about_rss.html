Subscribe to the Globus Toolkit's RSS (Really Simple Syndication) feed to get news about downloads and other timely GT information delivered directly to you via an RSS Aggregator. RSS is an XML-based format for sharing and distributing Web content, such as news headlines. Using an RSS reader, you can view data feeds from various news sources and weblogs, including headlines, summaries, and links to full stories.

To view the feed in your RSS Aggregator

  1. Copy the URL of the XML icon (either click on the icon and copy the URL or right click/option click and choose 'Copy link location').
  2. Paste the URL into your reader. (For some aggregators, you can simply right click/option click and 'bookmark' the feed to a special folder)

RSS Aggregators

RSS/News Aggregators (also called Readers) will download and display RSS feeds for you. A number of free and commercial News Aggregators are available for download. Simply do a search on Google (or try Sage for Firefox browsers).

Many aggregators are separate, "stand-alone" programs; other services will let you add RSS feeds to a Web page.
